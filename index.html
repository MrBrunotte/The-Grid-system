How to use the grid system


Since a row is divided into 12 every combination that fits into 12 works on one row.


Offset and less than 12

If we have a span that is less than 12 we have to offset it if we want it closer to the right of the screen.

Offset - position

Wrapped elements > 12

If we divid a row into more than 12, say 16, we get a wrapped row. Here we have col-4 four times (4x4=16) within a [class="row"]. Only three col-4 (3x4=12) fits in one row! so one grid-element is wrapped
